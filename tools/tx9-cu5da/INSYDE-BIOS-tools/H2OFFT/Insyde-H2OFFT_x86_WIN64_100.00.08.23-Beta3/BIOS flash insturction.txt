[Preparation]
Please decompress the compressed document under "Flash Tool".
Please copy BIOS Binary xxx.bin into the decompressed folder.
Please copy the decompressed folder to the corresponding OS environment.

[Command under Shell]
   H20FFT-Sx64.efi xxx.bin -ALL -SSB 

[Command under DOS]
   H2OFFT-D.exe xxx.bin -ALL -SSB

[Command under Windows]
   H2OFFT-Wx64.exe xxx.bin -ALL -SSB

[Command under Linux]
   ./H2OFFTx64.sh xxx.bin -ALL -SSB

[Enhancement]
-ALL -SSB         	Update the whole flash except BIOS password
-ALL -ME		    Update ME region
-ALL -BIOS       	Update BIOS region except NVRAM
-ALL -BIOS -SSB 	Update BIOS region
-N                  	disable auto reboot
